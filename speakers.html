SPEAKERS





SMRITI NAGPAL

Philanthropy, Sign language interpreter, Founder - Atulyakala


Smriti Nagpal is a true humanitarian and a successful entrepreneur. She presented the morning news bulletin for the hearing impaired at Doordarshan, rendering a mainstream mode of news dissemination, a much more pluralistic and inclusive character. She has constructed a creative and expressive landscape for the deaf community, through her startup “Atulyakala” which generated valuable job opportunities as well as the “Hearken Café” that she has co-founded. She is the recipient of numerous prestigious accolades. The amalgam of her entrepreneurial and Humanitarian endeavors earned her a position in BBC's '100 Women' and '30 Under 30' which is a testament to the philanthropic and loving nature of her work and an inspiration for Many.





ADITYA ARORA

Tech, Entrepreneur


“Age is just a number, but what you do with that number is what matters’’, how rightly put are these lines and the hard work they bring out is just commendable. Aditya Arora, the CEO of Faad Network Private Limited, and a very successful entrepreneur has done it all at such a ripe age, his self-belief and constant hard-work helped him win many accolades like REX Paramveer chakra, Indian Parliamentary meritorious award, Indian achievers award and he was also nominated for Padma Shree 2020. Having represented India at prestigious forums like, “21st World Business Dialogue in Germany”, “Harvard Project for Asia and International Relations”, and “World Startup Fest”, Aditya has made his voice heard among the audience by conducting almost 150+ events in the last 3 years. His innate desire to help underprivileged people led him to start ‘The Education Yatra campaign’- A Social Campaign that aimed to provide underprivileged children with the right to the right education. He has inspired many budding entrepreneurs to believe that they can aspire for success no matter what their age is, what matters is the sheer determination they have to invest.





ISHA YADAV

Professor, JMC (DU); Founder - Delhi Art Slam


Isha Yadav redefined the slam culture in 2017 with her brainchild ‘Delhi Art Slam’. It is a living and breathing community, as well as the first endeavour in the world to introduce art to slam culture. Her interests and concerns are not only limited to the expressive and performative arts, but also extend to humanitarian causes and initiatives. She collaborated with ‘The Education Tree’ to generate awareness about mental illness. She is also working on an online expository drive which goes by the name of ‘Museum of Rape Threats & Sexism’, that seeks to bring into light the deep rooted and pernicious attitudes, outlooks, actions and perspectives of the perpetrators. She is a poet and a feminist. She describes herself as a passionate and dynamic individual who believes that one is meant for greater things.





LOKESH VERMA

Founder- Heartwork Tattoo Festival; Tattoo Artist, International celebrity


Founder- Heartwork Tattoo Festival; Tattoo Artist, International celebrity India's first Award-winning, internationally experienced artist Lokesh is a veteran tattoo artist who was also one of the first people to start tattooing in India. Lokesh Verma is well known for the terrific amalgamation of varied tattoo styles but is best known for his coloured portraits. He is the first artist in India to start coloured realism and portraits style of tattooing that is mastered by only a few people across the world. He is best known for pioneering sound wave tattoos in India. Verma with his zeal to colour the blank canvas of life has attained the titles of many ‘firsts’- from being the first Asian to teach tattooing at an Italian University or be it the curation of HeartWork Tattoo Festival- India’s only international tattoo festival. A veteran artist, Lokesh has many feathers in his cap from winning the first prize in Inter Asia level painting in Japan to holding a Guinness World record for inking maximum number of flags to be tattooed on the human body! But the journey has not been easy for him. He began his pursuit at nineteen. An MBA by qualification, he strove hard to purchase the gear, even taking up a part-time position of a DJ at a local bar. Self-taught artist Lokesh Verma has added many layers into this industry by coming up with one of the most renowned brand of tattoo studios titled as, Devil’z Tattooz spanning across Delhi & Gurgaon. He is the trailblazer of tattoo community and is nothing short of a mastermind.





SUJATA SAHU

Social Entrepreneur, Founder- 17000 ft


Sujata Sahu or as people call her the “Iron Lady of Ladakh” is a social entrepreneur and a true samaritan. She started as a corporate techie and now works as a teacher. She believes that the duty of a teacher is not only confined to the four walls of an institution but are beyond that. Ms. Sahu didn’t let this spirit deter even in the strenuous conditions in Ladakh. She single handedly put forward the foundation stone of 17000 ft- A non- government organisation which has provided playgrounds to 140 schools and donated libraries to 230 schools giving books in Hindi, English and Urdu. This 17000 ft. foundation today works in over 220 viilage schools of Ladakh, Leh and Kargil to improve education,empower communities and create opportunities for their development. Ms. Sahu even won the most coveted Nari Shakti Puraskar in 2015 and Women Transforming India Award in 2019 for her unwavering conviction to give back to the people. Her undying passion and spirit to work for the betterment of the society is an example for all the young minds today.





HEENA GAMBHIR

Journalist


Ms. Heena Gambhir is a steadfast, perseverant and celebrated journalist. With a career that expands well over thirteen years in journalism, the richness of her experience in the field is a rightfully achieved artefact. She started her career as a news anchor at Doordarshan News in 2007, and then worked at NDTV for more than 7 years. She’s currently working with the Times network as a news anchor and a special correspondent with specialization in Finance, Civil Aviation, Government Policy, Energy sector and environment related issues which is a testament to the wide and overarching expertise that she holds in the forementioned spaces.
